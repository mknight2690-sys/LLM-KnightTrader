  6. Wait for the status to show "Connected" (green)
  7. Check which country you landed on. If it's one of these, you're good — skip to step 10:
    • ✅ Netherlands, ✅ Switzerland, ✅ Poland, ✅ Romania, ✅ Norway, ✅ Japan
  8. If you landed on a BLOCKED country (USA, Canada, Singapore, etc.), you need to switch:
🕒 SERVER-SWITCHING PROCEDURE (follow exactly):
  1. ATTEMPT 1: After your first "Connect", watch the on-screen timer/counter. Wait until it reaches 1 minute 30 seconds (1:30). Then click "Change Server"
  2. Check the country. If it's Netherlands, Switzerland, Poland, Romania, Norway, or Japan → you're done! Skip to step 10.
  3. ATTEMPT 2: If still on a blocked country, wait until the counter reaches 1 minute 40 seconds (1:40) from your initial connect. Then click "Change Server" again
  4. Check the country. If it's an allowed country → done! Skip to step 10.
  5. ATTEMPT 3: If still blocked, wait until the counter reaches 1 minute 50 seconds (1:50) from your initial connect. Then click "Change Server" one more time
  6. Check the country. By the 3rd attempt you should land on an allowed country.

What counter? The ProtonVPN app shows a connection duration timer on the home screen (e.g. "0:42"). That's what you watch. You're timing from when you first hit Connect.

  1. Confirm your country is one of: Netherlands, Switzerland, Poland, Romania, Norway, or Japan
DO NOT stay on these countries — they are on BloFin's blocked list:
❌ United States, ❌ Canada, ❌ Singapore

5
Verify Your Identity (KYC)

BloFin requires identity verification before you can create API keys or trade.

Make sure ProtonVPN is connected first (if using one).

  1. On the BloFin website, click your profile icon (top-right corner)
  2. Click "Identification" or "Verify Identity"
  3. Where it says "Enter real name", type your legal first and last name exactly as it appears on your ID card
  4. Select ID Type: "ID Card" (or "Passport" if that's what you have)
  5. Enter your ID Card number (the number printed on your physical ID card)
  6. Select the Date of issue of your ID card from the calendar
  7. Select the Date of expiry of your ID card from the calendar
  8. Click "Next"
  9. If prompted for face verification: follow on-screen instructions (you'll use your phone's camera)
  10. When the form is complete, click "Done" and wait for verification (usually takes a few minutes, sometimes up to 24 hours)
Your name, ID number, date of issue, and date of expiry must exactly match your physical ID card. Even small typos will cause verification to fail.
6
Create BloFin API Keys

LLM KnightTrader needs API keys to connect to BloFin and place trades.

Make sure ProtonVPN is connected first (if using one).

  1. Go to: blofin.com/account/apis
    • If that link doesn't work: go to blofin.com → click your profile icon (top-right) → click "API"
  2. Click "Create API Key"
  3. Fill in the form:
    • API Key Name: type anything, e.g. MyBot
    • Description: optional, leave blank or write "trading bot"
    • Permissions: check only "Trade". NEVER enable "Withdraw".
    • Passphrase: create a new password just for this API key — save it in your text file
  4. Click "Confirm"
  5. If prompted for 2FA, enter the code from your authenticator app or SMS/email
  6. ⚠️ IMMEDIATELY copy and save all THREE of these:
    • API Key (looks like: abc123def456...)
    • Secret Key (looks like: xyz789...) — only shown ONCE!
    • Passphrase (the password you just created for this API key)
IMPORTANT: The Secret Key is only shown ONCE on this page. Copy it immediately. If you close this page without saving it, you'll need to delete the key and create a new one.
7
Fund Your BloFin Account — Buy USDT

LLM KnightTrader trades USDT-margined futures, so you need USDT (Tether) on BloFin to get started. USDT is a "stablecoin" — 1 USDT = 1 USD.

Make sure ProtonVPN is connected first (if using one).

There are several ways to get USDT. Pick one of the options below:

━━━ OPTION A: Buy directly on BloFin (Easiest — stays in one place) ━━━

BloFin has a built-in "Buy Crypto" feature that accepts credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay through their payment partners (Simplex, Alchemy Pay, Checkout.com).

  1. Log in to your BloFin account
  2. Hover over "Assets" in the top navigation bar
  3. Click "Overview" → then click the "Buy Crypto" button
  4. Spend currency: select your local currency (USD, EUR, etc.)
  5. Receive currency: select USDT
  6. Enter the amount you want to spend (minimum ~$15)
  7. Payment method: choose "Card" (credit/debit) or "Apple Pay" / "Google Pay"
  8. Click "Buy Now"
  9. Review the order details, check the box, click "Pay"
  10. You'll be redirected to their payment partner to enter your card details and verify your identity
  11. Once complete, your USDT arrives in your BloFin Funding Account (usually within minutes)
Fees: Expect ~2-4% in fees when buying by card. It's the fastest way but costs slightly more. If your card is declined, try Apple Pay or Google Pay instead.

━━━ OPTION B: Cash App → BloFin (Good for US users) ━━━

Cash App lets you buy Bitcoin instantly with a linked debit card, then send it anywhere.

  1. Download Cash App from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Create an account and link your debit card
  3. Complete identity verification (they'll ask for your name, date of birth, and last 4 digits of SSN)
  4. Tap the "Bitcoin" tab on the home screen
  5. Tap "Buy Bitcoin" → enter the amount in USD (e.g. $50)
  6. Tap "Buy Now" and confirm with your PIN/fingerprint
  7. Leave the Bitcoin in Cash App for now — you'll send it to BloFin in the next step
  8. Why not buy USDT directly? Cash App only supports Bitcoin. You'll convert it to USDT on BloFin after.

━━━ OPTION C: Coinbase → BloFin (Good outside the US, lower fees) ━━━

Coinbase is available in 100+ countries and lets you buy USDT directly with your local currency.

  1. Go to: coinbase.com and create an account
  2. Complete identity verification (photo ID scan + selfie)
  3. Click "Buy & Sell" at the top
  4. Buy: select USDT (Tether)
  5. Enter your amount (e.g. $50 worth)
  6. Add a payment method: debit card (instant, ~3.99% fee), bank transfer (3-5 days, lower fee), or PayPal (US/UK only, ~2-3% fee)
  7. Click "Buy Now"
  8. Your USDT is now in your Coinbase account — ready to send to BloFin

━━━ OPTION D: Kraken → BloFin (Good for EU/UK, lowest fees) ━━━

Kraken has some of the lowest fees and supports SEPA bank transfers (free) in Europe.

  1. Go to: kraken.com and create an account
  2. Complete identity verification (photo ID)
  3. Click "Buy Crypto" in the top navigation
  4. Select USDT
  5. For EU users: select "Bank Transfer (SEPA)" for free deposits (arrives in 1-2 days)
  6. For everyone else: debit card is instant (~3-4% fee)
  7. Once your USDT arrives, you can send it to BloFin

━━━ OPTION E: PayPal → BloFin (Only if you already have a balance) ━━━

  • US users: PayPal lets you buy crypto directly in the app. However, you cannot send PayPal-purchased crypto to BloFin — it stays in your PayPal wallet.
  • Workaround: Use PayPal to fund Coinbase or Kraken (select PayPal as payment method), buy USDT there, then send to BloFin.
  • Go to Coinbase or Kraken, add payment method, select "PayPal", buy USDT.

━━━ NEXT: If you bought USDT on another exchange (Coinbase, Kraken, Cash App) — Send it to BloFin ━━━

If you used Option A (bought directly on BloFin), your USDT is already there. Skip to Step 8.

If you used Options B, C, or D, you need to send your USDT to BloFin. Follow these steps carefully:

  1. On BloFin: log in → hover over "Assets" (top-right) → click "Deposit"
  2. Search for and select USDT
  3. Choose the network: select "TRON (TRC20)"
    • ✅ TRC20 is the fastest and cheapest (~$1 fee). Use this for Cash App and most exchanges.
    • ✅ ERC20 (Ethereum) works too but costs $5-15 in gas fees.
    • ⚠️ CRITICAL: If using Cash App, you're sending Bitcoin — select "Bitcoin" and "BTC" network instead of USDT/TRC20.
  4. Copy the deposit address (the long string of letters and numbers)
  5. On your other exchange/Cash App: go to "Withdraw" or "Send"
    • Cash App: tap Bitcoin → tap "Send" → paste the BloFin BTC deposit address → enter amount → send
    • Coinbase: click "Send/Receive" → "Send" → select USDT → paste address → select TRC20 network → send
    • Kraken: click "Withdraw" → select USDT → paste address → select TRON network → send
  6. ⚠️ DOUBLE-CHECK THE NETWORK: The network you select on BloFin (when copying the address) MUST match the network you select on the sending platform. If they don't match, your funds will be lost forever.
  7. Wait for the deposit to arrive (usually 1-5 minutes for crypto, up to 24 hours for bank transfers)
  8. On BloFin, check under Assets → Overview — your USDT balance should appear
⚠️ CRITICAL: Understand "USDT-Margined Futures"

LLM KnightTrader trades futures contracts that are settled in USDT, not in Bitcoin or dollars. This means:

  • You need USDT on BloFin — not BTC, not ETH
  • Your profit and loss is measured in USDT
  • If you sent BTC from Cash App, you need to convert it to USDT on BloFin first (click "Trade" → "Spot" → trade BTC/USDT)

Troubleshooting

"403 Forbidden" or "geo-restricted" error from BloFin:

  • Your ProtonVPN is either not connected or connected to a blocked country. Reconnect to Netherlands or Switzerland and try again.

BloFin signup says "country not allowed":

  • Connect to ProtonVPN first (Netherlands recommended), then refresh BloFin.

"No module named 'trader'" error:

  • You didn't activate the virtual environment. In your terminal, run: .venv\Scripts\activate then retry.

"Trader offline" in dashboard after starting:

  • Wait 10 more seconds — the trader takes a moment to connect. If still offline after 30s, check the error log at data\logs\trader.err (if the file exists).

"ModuleNotFoundError" during pip install:

  • You need Python 3.12 or newer. Download from python.org and reinstall (check "Add Python to PATH" in the installer).

Desktop shortcuts not appearing:

  • In Cursor's terminal, run: powershell -NoProfile -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File scripts\create_desktop_shortcuts.ps1
